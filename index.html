AKATY

The Cardboard Vacuum Cleaner

A real, working little sweeper built by hand from a recycled box, a battery, a motor, and a lot of glitter tape. Flip the switch and the brush starts to spin.

01

How it works

  1. Switch on. Flip the red I/O switch on the side.
  2. Power flows. The AA battery pushes electricity through the wires.
  3. Motor spins. The little motor starts turning fast.
  4. Brush sweeps. The motor spins the bristle roller, and it sweeps dust and bits up into the box as you push AKATY along.
02

Look inside

Compartment · Control

The power switch

A red rocker switch marked I/O sits on the outside wall. Press it to start and stop the whole machine.

Compartment · Engine bay

Battery & motor

An AA battery in a holder feeds a small DC motor through red and blue wires. This is the part that makes everything move.

Compartment · Sweeper

The brush roller

A wooden dowel wrapped with red glitter-foam bristles. The motor spins it so it flicks dust and small bits up off the floor.

Compartment · Detail

The side cap

A blue bottle cap, taped neatly to the side, finishes the body and gives AKATY its own little face.
